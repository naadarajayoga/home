Refund Policy

At Naada Raja Yoga, we maintain a strict no-refund policy to ensure fairness and clarity for all participants.

No Refunds on Purchases

Once you purchase a class, subscription, or any service, it is non-refundable, regardless of whether you attend or use the service.

No Refunds for Cancellations or Missed Classes

If you cancel your participation, do not attend, or miss classes, you are not eligible for a refund.

No Refunds for Subscription Terminations

Subscription cancellations (by you or due to failed payments) do not qualify for refunds.

Exceptions

Refunds will only be considered if a session or service is cancelled by Naada Raja Yoga and cannot be rescheduled.

Responsibility of Participants

You are responsible for ensuring you can attend the classes you book and for having the necessary internet connection and device to access online sessions.
