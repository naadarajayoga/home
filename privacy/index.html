Privacy Policy

At Naada Raja Yoga, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We only collect minimal information necessary for providing our services, such as:

  • Contact details you choose to share with us (e.g., via email, contact form, or messaging platforms).
  • Information about how you reach us (e.g., through Facebook, email, or other platforms).
  • Payment details are securely processed by third-party providers such as PayPal and are never stored by us.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Providing and improving our yoga classes and services.
  • Communicating with you regarding classes, updates, or support.
  • Maintaining transaction records where applicable.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Information may only be shared when legally required or with trusted third-party services (e.g., payment processors) to complete transactions.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no online method of transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for future communications.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact Page.
